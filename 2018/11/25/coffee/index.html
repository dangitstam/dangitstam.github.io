Seattle Coffee Shops

Tam Dang
2018-11-25

I spend most of my time in coffee shops and it’s starting to get too difficult keeping track of all of them. Here are my favorite coffee shops to study at in no particular order.

Victrola (Pike St.)

Hours — 6:30 AM - 8:00 PM (Weekdays)   |   7:30 AM - 8:00 PM (Weekends)
Bus — From UW, Route 49 on 42nd & 15th.
Pros

  • In the heart of Capitol Hill
  • Excellent espresso
  • Plenty of tables
  • Plenty of outlets
  • Great pastries, food if you need it
  • Roastery (fresh beans)

Cons

  • Crowded on weekends

This one is easier to get to than the one on 15th, and has more natural light.

Ballard Coffee Works

Hours — 6:30 AM - 9:00 PM (MTWThF)   |   7:00 AM - 9:00 PM (Sat)   |   7:00 AM - 8:00 PM (Sun)
Bus — Route 44 on 45th and Brooklyn
Pros

  • In the heart of Ballard
  • Excellent espresso
  • Plenty of tables
  • Plenty of outlets
  • Okay pastries, food if you need it
  • Roastery (fresh beans)

Cons

  • Crowded almost always (not to the point of not getting a table though)

The staff is knowledgeable and they have a lot of coffee tech on sale (chemex, aeropress, etc.).

Seven Market & Coffee

Hours — 7:00 AM - 8:00 PM (Always)
Bus — Route 74 toward Ravenna
Pros

  • In a quiet part of Ravenna (very chill part of Seattle in general)
  • Excellent espresso
  • Good cold brew (growlers available)
  • Decent pastries, food if you need it
  • Roastery (fresh beans)

Cons

  • Crowded almost always (coming here is a gamble)

Don’t come here on Sundays, it will be full of people who put off doing their homework during the week.
The market also has plenty of beer, wine, cider, and local goods (even eggs and milk).

Zoka (Greenlake)

Hours — 6:00 AM - 9:00 PM (MTWThf - Sat)   |   6:00 AM - 7:00 PM (Sun)
Bus — Route 45 toward Loyal Heights and 62 toward Greenlake
Pros

  • Great espresso
  • Excellent cold brew (growlers available)
  • Plenty of tables
  • Excellent pastries, decent food if you need it
  • Roastery (fresh beans)

Cons

  • Crowded on weekends
  • Outlets are only on the tables along the edges + in the study space toward the restrooms

It’s at its busiest on Saturday morning and there isn’t a whole lot immediately surrounding it.
